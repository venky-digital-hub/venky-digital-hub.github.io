## 2. Core Intralogistics Solutions

### ASRS Pallets for Automated Warehouses
As modern supply chains transition toward warehouse automation, high-bay storage facilities demand zero-tolerance physical infrastructure. Mekins specializes in manufacturing ASRS-compatible pallets designed specifically for robotic storage systems, high-speed shuttles, and automated material handling environments. These units are engineered for absolute dimensional accuracy and minimal deflection under load, preventing sensor faults and crane jams.

### Cage Pallets for Secure Material Handling
Ideal for storing and transporting loose components, sub-assemblies, and finished goods, industrial cage pallets combine a rigid steel base with high-strength perimeter walls. This structural configuration offers total content visibility and protection while enabling safe, multi-tiered stacking.

### Heavy-Duty Steel vs. Conventional Wood
For heavy manufacturing sectors, replacing legacy wooden assets with fabricated steel infrastructure radically alters the **Total Cost of Ownership (TCO)**:

| Operational Metric | Conventional Wooden Pallets | Mekins Heavy-Duty Steel Pallets |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Structural Strength** | Low to Moderate (Deforms under stress) | Superior high-tonnage load stability |
| **Service Lifespan** | Short (High recurring replacement rate) | Multi-decade operational durability |
| **Fire Safety Profile** | High combustible fuel load | 100% Fire-resistant / Non-combustible |
| **Sanitization** | Porous (Absorbs moisture, oils & bacteria) | Non-porous, easy industrial washdown |
| **System Debris** | Splinters & loose nails jam conveyors | Zero fragmentation or shedding |
