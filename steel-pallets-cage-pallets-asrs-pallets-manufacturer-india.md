## ASRS Pallets for Automated Warehouses

As industries move toward warehouse automation, the demand for **ASRS pallets** is increasing.

ASRS stands for **Automated Storage and Retrieval System**. These systems are commonly used in high-bay warehouses, robotic storage systems and automated distribution centres.

For an ASRS system to work smoothly, pallets must be strong, accurate and consistent.

ASRS pallets are designed for:

- Dimensional accuracy
- Load stability
- Conveyor compatibility
- Crane and stacker handling
- Repeated automated movement
- High-bay storage
- Long service life

Mekins manufactures ASRS-compatible pallets that support smooth material movement inside automated warehouse systems.

For companies planning warehouse automation, choosing the right ASRS pallet is an important step toward better productivity and efficiency.

## Cage Pallets for Secure Storage and Transportation

Cage pallets are ideal for industries that need secure storage and safe transportation of components, assemblies and finished goods.

Unlike flat pallets, cage pallets come with steel cage walls or mesh sides. This helps protect materials during handling, stacking and movement.

Cage pallets are useful for:

- Component storage
- Finished goods movement
- Returnable logistics
- Warehouse organization
- Secure transportation
- Stackable storage
- Better product visibility

Mekins cage pallets are designed with robust steel construction, making them suitable for repeated industrial use.
